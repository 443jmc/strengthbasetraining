Personal Training For Your Relationship

Strength-based relationship training takes you from the relationship you have to the relationship you want to have.

Apply to Start Training See How Sessions Work

The Approach

Training, Not Just Talking

A relationship trainer works the way a strength coach works: showing you the movement, watching you attempt it, and correcting your form — session after session, rep after rep — until the new way of showing up is the way you actually show up.

Every session follows the same structure, and every session ends with work to do before the next one. This isn't a place to vent for an hour and feel better. It's a place to build a capacity you don't have yet.

The Format

A Relationship Training Session Has Three Parts

Every session moves through the same three components, in order.

01

Skill Demonstration

Your personal trainer will demonstrate what it looks like to make contact and tell the truth in the kindest way possible.

02

Behavioral Observation

Your personal trainer will observe how you treat each other and share those observations with you.

03

Skill Replication

Your personal trainer will ask each of you to practice making contact and telling each other what you really think in the kindest way possible.

Between Sessions

The Work Continues After You Leave

After every session, your personal relationship trainer will give you a written assignment which you are to turn in before your next session.

These assignments are not optional, and must be turned in before you attend your next session.

If you are willing to tolerate the discomfort of growth, you will discover a new capacity for courage and kindness that will improve your life dramatically.
